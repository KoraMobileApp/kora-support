Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Kora Mobile App Privacy Policy. This document explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data.

Data Collection

  • What we collect: Your name, email address, and Google account profile picture for personalization.
  • How it's used: To deliver a seamless and personalized user experience.

Data Storage and Security

  • Your chat history is securely stored in Firebase with industry-standard encryption.
  • We use HTTPS for secure communication between the app and servers.

Third-Party Services

  • We integrate with OpenAI's API to process inquiries. OpenAI does not use your chat data for training.
  • Firebase is used for secure data storage and management.

Your Rights

  • You can delete your data at any time through the app's settings.
  • We comply with GDPR and CCPA regulations to ensure your privacy is safeguarded.
